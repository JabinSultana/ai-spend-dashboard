# Economics

## Estimated Lead Value

A converted lead could be valuable to Credex if the company helps businesses reduce AI infrastructure or tooling costs. For example, if a startup saves thousands of dollars annually through optimized AI spending and credits, Credex could potentially earn revenue through consulting, partnerships, or infrastructure usage.

Assuming:

- Average successful customer value: $2,000–$10,000 annually
- Consultation conversion rate: 5–10%
- Audit-to-lead conversion rate: 20–40%

Even a relatively small number of high-quality leads could make the tool financially valuable.

---

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

The initial strategy focuses on low-cost organic distribution:

- Twitter/X posts
- Hacker News launches
- Reddit discussions
- Founder communities
- Shareable audit reports

Estimated early-stage CAC:

- Organic traffic: near $0
- Community outreach: low time cost
- Paid acquisition (future): potentially $10–$50 per qualified lead

---

## Conversion Funnel Assumptions

Example funnel:

- 1,000 visitors
- 400 completed audits
- 120 captured emails
- 15 consultation calls
- 3 paying customers

This would already demonstrate meaningful conversion potential for a free lead-generation product.

---

## Path to $1M ARR

To reach $1M ARR within 18 months:

- The tool would need strong organic distribution
- Viral sharing through public audit links
- Consistent founder/startup adoption
- High-value infrastructure or consulting conversions

Example:

- 100 paying customers
- Average annual value: $10,000

This alone could generate $1M ARR.

---

## Important Assumption

These numbers are rough estimates intended to model business viability, not exact financial forecasts.

---

## Why This Model Could Work

The strongest advantage of this product is that it targets companies already spending money on AI tools and infrastructure. Instead of convincing users to adopt a completely new workflow, the tool focuses on reducing existing costs, which makes the value proposition easier to understand and potentially easier to convert.
